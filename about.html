What is Noumenal Sky?

Noumenal Sky is an educational website that aims to teach users some interesting facts about the objects within our solar system.

How do I use this?

If you come across something you have never heard of and don't understand; jargon such as albedo, or perihelion, simply click on them and a window will appear that will hopefully assist in making clear what those concepts are and what they mean.

Now what?

We hope you have fun learning with our website and it succeeds in teaching you some interesting things about our universe!
